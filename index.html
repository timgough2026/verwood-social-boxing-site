About Us

Verwood Social Boxing Club is a community‑focused fitness project designed to help people of all ages and abilities discover the benefits of boxing in a friendly, non‑competitive environment. Our classes combine cardio, strength, and coordination drills with social interaction and fun. You won’t spar unless you want to – our focus is on fitness, confidence and camaraderie.

Meet the Coach

Hi, I’m Tim – a local boxing enthusiast and the founder of Verwood Social Boxing Club. After years of training and running informal sessions for friends and neighbours, I decided to share my passion with the wider community. I’m currently undertaking a Level 3 Personal Trainer diploma with Study Active and working through the Hatton Academy’s Fundamentals & Advanced boxing fitness course. These qualifications will ensure our programmes are safe, effective and rooted in best practice. When I’m not coaching, you’ll find me enjoying the beautiful Dorset countryside with my family.

Boxing Accreditation Course

Earn your Verwood Boxing accreditation through our structured course. Progress through levels, master techniques and earn exclusive club swag.

  1. Level 1 – Fundamentals: Learn the basics of stance, guard and the four main punches. Complete the basics checklist to advance.
  2. Level 2 – Combinations & Conditioning: Master combinations and improve your endurance. Pass a fitness test to proceed.
  3. Level 3 – Sparring Skills: Optional controlled sparring sessions to apply your skills. Coaches will assess technique and ring craft.
  4. Level 4 – Coaching Assistant: Help coach a class under supervision. Develop leadership skills and build confidence.

Graduates receive a Verwood Social Boxing T‑shirt and certificate. Top performers can earn further rewards such as gloves and personalised gear.

Social Boxing – More Info

Our Social Boxing class is designed to be the perfect introduction to boxing. We combine pad work, footwork drills and bodyweight exercises in a supportive group setting. You’ll learn the basic punches and defensive moves while building cardiovascular fitness and strength. All abilities are welcome and no previous experience is required. Sessions run for one hour and are held indoors at Verwood Memorial Hall or outdoors at Potterne Park when the weather allows.

Youth BoxFit – More Info

Our Youth BoxFit class provides a safe, engaging environment for young people aged 10–16 to get active and learn boxing skills. Classes focus on discipline, coordination and confidence, using non‑contact drills and games to keep things fun. Participants will improve their fitness, learn respect and self‑control, and make new friends. All sessions are supervised by qualified coaches with safeguarding checks in place.

Outdoor Bootcamps – More Info

During spring and summer we take our training outside. These bootcamp sessions blend boxing drills with functional strength and agility exercises, taking advantage of Verwood’s parks and open spaces. Expect high‑energy circuits, partner drills and bodyweight movements that build endurance and resilience. Bootcamps are suitable for all fitness levels – our coaches will tailor the intensity to suit you.
