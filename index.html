About Us

Verwood Social Boxing Club is a community‑focused fitness project designed to help people of all ages and abilities discover the benefits of boxing in a friendly, non‑competitive environment. Our classes combine cardio, strength, and coordination drills with social interaction and fun. You won’t spar unless you want to – our focus is on fitness, confidence and camaraderie.

Our Classes

Social Boxing

A dynamic group workout featuring pad work, footwork drills and bodyweight exercises. Perfect for beginners and those looking to have fun and get fit.

Youth BoxFit

Engaging sessions for ages 10–16 that build confidence, discipline and coordination. Focused on technique and fitness without full contact sparring.

Outdoor Bootcamps

Spring and summer bootcamps held in Verwood’s parks. High‑energy sessions combining boxing drills with functional strength and agility circuits.
